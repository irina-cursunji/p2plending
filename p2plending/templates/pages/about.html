About Us

Bridge Capital was founded in January 2024 in Rotterdam by an ambitious Erasmus School of Economics student, Maxwell Koulen. Maxwell dreams of a future where finance truly benefits society through both innovation and social mobility.

Bridge Capital has since facilitated more than $3.5 billion in loans to over 180,000 people. Through our platform, people can invest in each other in a way that is both financially and socially rewarding. Borrowers apply online for a fixed-rate, fixed-term loan between $2,000 and $5,000,000. Individuals and institutions can invest in the loans and earn attractive returns. Bridge Capital handles all loan servicing on behalf of the matched borrowers and investors.

Bridge Capital's marketplace is backed by leading investors including Sequoia Capital, Francisco Partners, Institutional Venture Partners, and Credit Suisse NEXT Fund.
